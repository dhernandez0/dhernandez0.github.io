Dr. Daniel Hernandez Juarez is a Deep Learning Research Scientist at SLAMcore.

He received his Ph.D. in Computer Vision (2020) from the Universitat Autonoma de Barcelona, under the supervision of Dr. Juan Carlos Moure and Dr. David VÃ¡zquez, at the Computer Architecture and Operating Systems (UAB department) in Barcelona. The focus of his thesis was to improve 3D perception algorithms and adapt them to GPU devices. He has done internships at Daimler AG and Element AI.

He is an active member of SLAMcore's research team in London, where he works on topics at the intersection of deep learning, computer vision and robotics.

His research interests include:

  • Computer Vision: Color Constancy, Depth Completion, Stixel World and 3D Perception.

  • Robotics: Autonomous Driving, SLAM.

  • HPC: Parallel Computing, CUDA, GPGPU and real-time Algorithms.
